About Me

The longer version.

I'm a Computational Biologist working in ML for Computational Pathology at Human Technopole in Milan, Italy. I build tools and models that sit at the intersection of biology and data, from genomic sequence modeling to autonomous ML experimentation.

My work spans the full stack of computational biology: data pipelines, deep learning models and downstream interpretation. Right now I'm particularly interested in how AI agents can accelerate the research loop. I want to replace the manual iteration that makes ML research slow.

Outside of work I'm an average athlete with an above average obsession with training data. I run, lift, and fight. I collect data and think how to use it.

I write about science, biotech, and the navigation of early careers in fast-moving fields on Substack.
